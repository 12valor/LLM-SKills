# Non-AI Color Palettes by Aesthetic School

Use these as starting points. Modify — never copy verbatim.

---

## Brutalist / Raw
- Ink: `#0A0A0A`
- Paper: `#F5F0E8`
- Accent: `#FF3300` or `#FFCC00`
- Mid: `#C8C0B4`
- Vibe: Hard shadows, no radius, type as decoration

## Japandi / Restrained
- Ink: `#1C1917`
- Paper: `#FAF7F2`
- Accent: `#8B7355` (warm clay)
- Mid: `#D6CFC4`
- Vibe: Enormous whitespace, no decoration, single accent use

## Editorial / Magazine
- Ink: `#111111`
- Paper: `#FFFDF7`
- Accent: `#C41230` (deep red)
- Mid: `#888`
- Vibe: Type-heavy, large contrasts, rules (lines) as decoration

## Industrial / Bauhaus
- Ink: `#1A1A2E`
- Paper: `#E8E0D4`
- Accent: `#E63946`
- Secondary: `#457B9D`
- Vibe: Primary palette, geometric structure, function-first

## Warm Luxury
- Ink: `#2C1810`
- Paper: `#FDF6EC`
- Accent: `#B8860B` (dark goldenrod)
- Mid: `#C4A882`
- Vibe: Serif type, gold accents, generous padding

## Dark & Clinical
- Ink: `#E8EDF2`
- Paper: `#0D1117`
- Accent: `#00D4FF` (cyan)
- Mid: `#30363D`
- Vibe: Code-adjacent, monospace, low-contrast secondary info

## Organic / Earth
- Ink: `#2D2416`
- Paper: `#F4EFE6`
- Accent: `#4A7C59` (forest green)
- Mid: `#A89880`
- Vibe: Rounded shapes, botanical references, soft motion

## Retro Digital / 90s Web
- Ink: `#000080` (navy)
- Paper: `#C0C0C0` (silver) or `#FFFFFF`
- Accent: `#FF00FF` (fuchsia)
- Secondary: `#00FF00` (lime)
- Vibe: Pixel borders, dithering, bitmap fonts, nostalgia

## Art Deco
- Ink: `#1A1209`
- Paper: `#F5EFD6`
- Accent: `#C9A84C` (gold)
- Secondary: `#2E5B4A` (forest)
- Vibe: Geometric ornament, symmetry, monumental scale
